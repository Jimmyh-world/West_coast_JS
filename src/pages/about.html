About WestCoast Education

Our Story

For over 40 years, WestCoast Education has been at the forefront of technical education, specializing in IT and development training. Located on Sweden's beautiful west coast north of Gothenburg, we've built our reputation on excellence in classroom and distance learning.

Our Expertise

We specialize in web and mobile development training, offering both traditional classroom experiences and cutting-edge distance learning options. Our courses are designed and led by industry professionals who bring real-world experience to the classroom.

Our Future

As we embrace the digital future, we're expanding our offerings to include on-demand courses while maintaining the high standards of education our students expect. This allows us to reach more students while providing flexible learning options.
